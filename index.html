I am a Researcher at the Manufacturing Engineering Department of Karabuk University. I am also working at the same position at the department of Mechatronics Engineering.


Brief story: I was born in Kocaeli/Turkey. My undergraduate degree was in Mechatronics Education at Suleyman Demirel University (SDU) and Universita degli studi dell'Aquila (Univaq) The Erasmus Exchange program allowed me to spend a semester and a summer term in Italy. I then completed Master of Science of the Manufacturing Engineering at SDU, followed by a PhD in the same department at Karabuk University (KBU) I spent a 3 months as a visiting researcher at Politecnico di Milano where I studied on FEM and experimental based thread milling operation.
